# Privacy Policy
**TIBETAN LUNAR CALENDAR**

## LOCAL SANCTUARY & USER INFORMATION
All your data is stored strictly on your local device's LocalStorage. We do not have servers that store your personal information. To provide a personalized experience, the App stores:
- **Profile Details**: Dharma name, gender, and birth details (used for calculating lunar resonance).
- **Practitioner Logs**: Personal notes, reminders, and stickers attached to specific calendar dates.
- **Preferences**: Language and interface settings.

## DATA SOVEREIGNTY & REMOVAL
You have total control over your digital footprint. You can remove your data at any time via:
1. **In-App**: Use the **"Clear All System Cache"** option in the Profile/Settings section to instantly wipe all local data.
2. **Device Settings**: Clear your browser's "Local Storage" or "Site Data" for this app to remove all stored information.
3. **Uninstalling**: On mobile devices, deleting the App will also remove all locally cached practitioner data.

## THIRD PARTY SERVICES
We use standard web technologies. No identifiable data is permanently retained beyond the session.

## DEVICE PERMISSIONS
We request minimal device permissions to provide core functionality:
- **Notifications**: Used exclusively to send local alerts for upcoming festivals, sacred days, and your custom personal reminders. These are scheduled locally and do not involve remote servers.

## CONTACT
For any questions regarding the Tibetan Lunar Calendar, please reach out to the developer.
